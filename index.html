Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common questions our customers ask about our cleaning services

01 How much does your cleaning service cost?

Our pricing depends on the size of your space, the type of cleaning required, and the frequency of service. We offer free in-home estimates to provide you with an accurate quote.

02 How frequently should I schedule a cleaning?

This depends on your lifestyle and needs. Most clients opt for weekly or bi-weekly cleanings to maintain a consistently clean home. We also offer one-time deep cleans for special occasions or seasonal cleaning.

03 What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand that schedules change. You can cancel or reschedule your appointment with at least 24 hours notice without any penalty. For last-minute changes, we'll do our best to accommodate you.

04 Do I need to be home during cleaning?

It's not necessary for you to be home during our cleaning service. Many of our clients provide us with access to their homes via key lockboxes or garage codes. All our staff are thoroughly vetted and insured for your peace of mind.
